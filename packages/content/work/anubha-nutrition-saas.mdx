## Context

During **Nov 2025**–**Apr 2026** I worked as **Associate Full-Stack Engineer** at **Bitcode Softwares**. **Anubha Nutrition** (**anubhanutrition.in**) was a flagship deliverable: a **clinic** **and** **nutrition** **management** **SaaS** **for** **real** **operational** **use**.

## Problem

The organisation needed **digital** **workflows** **replacing** **fragmented** **processes** **—** **patient** **onboarding**, **nutrition** **planning** **support**, **scheduling**, **and** **staff** **efficiency**. **Downtime** **or** **data** **loss** **was** **unacceptable**.

## Solution

- **Next.js** **frontend** **with** **component** **discipline** **and** **performance** **awareness** **for** **mobile** **and** **desktop** **clinic** **staff**.
- **Node.js** **API** **layer** **with** **typed** **contracts** **and** **validated** **inputs**.
- **PostgreSQL** **+** **Prisma** **for** **relational** **integrity** **and** **migrations** **as** **the** **schema** **evolves**.
- **Docker** **for** **repeatable** **deploys**; **Nginx** **as** **TLS** **termination** **and** **reverse** **proxy**; **AWS** **EC2** **for** **hosting** **under** **our** **control**.

## Key decisions

**Prisma** **accelerated** **safe** **schema** **iteration** **with** **type-safe** **queries** **—** **critical** **when** **requirements** **shift** **with** **clinic** **feedback**. **Docker** **ensured** **dev** **parity** **and** **clean** **rollouts**. **Nginx** **+** **Let’s** **Encrypt**-class **TLS** **protected** **data** **in** **transit** **for** **users** **who** **access** **the** **system** **from** **multiple** **devices**.

## Result

**anubhanutrition.in** **runs** **as** **production** **SaaS** **with** **live** **users** **—** **evidence** **for** **any** **founder** **evaluating** **best** **software** **developer** **India** **candidates** **that** **I** **ship** **end-to-end** **with** **infra** **included**.
